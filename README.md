In certain stories, a White Rabbit appears   
not as an answer, but as an invitation.  

A faint signal, breaking through the noise.  
Something you can’t quite explain.  

It finds you, almost unnoticed.  
Whether you follow… is your choice.  

To follow is to accept what you don’t know,  
and step into the unknown.  

If you’ve found your way here,  
perhaps it has already begun.  

**Make your choice.**
